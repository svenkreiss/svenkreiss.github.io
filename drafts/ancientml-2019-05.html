AncientML 2: Steps Toward Artificial Intelligence (Minsky, 1961)

AncientML is a series of paper reading notes. The purpose is to review outstanding contributions to machine learning that are valuable to the formation as an academic field.

Steps Toward Artificial Intelligence (Minsky, 1961), PDF

Background: Marvin Minsky’s Wikipedia page.

  • “heuristic programming”: Search, Pattern Recognition, Learning, Planning, Induction
  • 1961: “… we are on the threshold of an era that will be strongly influenced, and quite possibly dominated, by intelligent problem-solving machines.”
